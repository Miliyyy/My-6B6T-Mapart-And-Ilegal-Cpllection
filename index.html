About

The Collection

A private archive of map art and rare/illegal items collected on the 6B6T.org anarchy Minecraft server. Each piece in this museum represents a unique moment, player, or event from the server's history.

CURATOR: Miliyyy
SERVER: 6B6T.org
TYPE: Anarchy / Survival
YEAR: 2026

Map Art

Map art is created by carefully placing blocks across the Minecraft terrain so that when viewed through an in-game map item, they form a pixel image. Each map is a unique handcrafted piece — some are solo works, others are large collaborative builds spanning dozens of chunks.

Illegal Items

On 6B6T, certain items exist outside the normal bounds of vanilla Minecraft — obtained through exploits, duplication glitches, or server-specific mechanics. These items are considered "illegal" by most server standards and are exceptionally rare to own and preserve.

Contact

Found something you recognize? Want to trade or share information about any of these items? Reach out in-game or on the 6B6T Discord.
